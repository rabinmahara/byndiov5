🏪 BYNDIO Seller Agreement

Version 1.0 | Effective: January 2025

This agreement governs your use of BYNDIO as a seller. By registering and listing products, you agree to all terms below.

1. Seller Eligibility

You must be 18+ years old, have a valid PAN card, and if GST registered, maintain a valid GSTIN. You are responsible for ensuring all products you list comply with Indian law.

2. Product Listings

  • Only list genuine, legal products you have rights to sell
  • Images and descriptions must be accurate and not misleading
  • Price must be your final price — no hidden fees to buyers
  • Counterfeit, prohibited, or adult products are strictly banned

3. Order Fulfilment

  • You must ship orders within 24 hours of receiving them
  • Use appropriate packaging to prevent transit damage
  • Provide accurate tracking information
  • Failure to fulfil 3+ orders may result in account suspension

4. Commission & Payments

  • BYNDIO charges 0% commission on sales
  • Platform fee of ₹10 per order applies to buyers (not sellers)
  • Payouts are processed weekly to your registered bank account
  • TDS at 1% is deducted on payouts exceeding ₹5 lakh per year per applicable Indian tax law

5. Returns & Refunds

  • Accept returns within 7 days for all products unless stated otherwise
  • Refunds are processed by BYNDIO on your behalf
  • Your payout will be adjusted for returned orders

6. Prohibited Conduct

  • Fake reviews on your own or competitors' products
  • Artificially inflating prices then offering fake "discounts"
  • Multiple seller accounts without BYNDIO approval
  • Direct transactions with buyers outside BYNDIO

7. Account Termination

BYNDIO may suspend or terminate your account for policy violations, fraud, or excessive negative buyer feedback. You may appeal to grievance@byndio.in within 30 days.

8. Governing Law

This agreement is governed by Indian law. Disputes are subject to jurisdiction of courts in Mumbai, Maharashtra.
